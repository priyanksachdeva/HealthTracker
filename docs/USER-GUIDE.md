
# Health Tracker User Guide

## Getting Started

Welcome to Health Tracker! This comprehensive guide will help you make the most of your health tracking journey.

## Installation

### Web App (PWA)
1. **Visit the App**: Open your browser and go to the Health Tracker website
2. **Install as App**: 
   - **Desktop**: Click the install icon (⊕) in your browser's address bar
   - **Mobile**: Tap "Add to Home Screen" when prompted
3. **Launch**: Access the app from your home screen or applications folder

### Android App
1. **Download**: Get the app from Google Play Store
2. **Install**: Follow standard installation process
3. **Permissions**: Grant required permissions for full functionality

## Main Features

### 📊 Dashboard (Home)
Your central hub for health overview and quick actions.

#### Today's Summary
- **Quick Stats**: Steps, calories, distance at a glance
- **Progress Rings**: Visual representation of daily goals
- **Weekly Trends**: 7-day overview of key metrics

#### Quick Actions
- **Log Weight**: Tap to record your current weight
- **Add Food**: Quick nutrition logging
- **Start Workout**: Begin activity tracking
- **View Goals**: Check and update your targets

### 🏃 Activity Tracking
Monitor your daily physical activities and exercise.

#### Daily Metrics
- **Steps**: Automatic counting (if device supports) or manual entry
- **Distance**: Calculated from steps or manually entered
- **Calories Burned**: Based on activity and personal data
- **Active Minutes**: Time spent in moderate to vigorous activity
- **Heart Rate**: Manual entry or device sync

#### How to Log Activity
1. **Automatic**: Enable device permissions for auto-tracking
2. **Manual Entry**:
   - Tap the "+" button on Activity page
   - Select activity type
   - Enter duration and intensity
   - Save your entry

#### Workout Tracking
1. **Start New Workout**:
   - Tap "Start Workout" 
   - Choose workout type (Running, Cycling, Strength, etc.)
   - Set timer if needed
2. **During Workout**:
   - Monitor elapsed time
   - Track heart rate (if available)
   - Pause/resume as needed
3. **End Workout**:
   - Stop timer
   - Review summary
   - Add notes if desired
   - Save workout

### 🎯 Goals Management
Set and track personal health objectives.

#### Setting Goals
1. **Navigate to Goals**: Tap Goals tab in bottom navigation
2. **Add New Goal**: Tap "+" button
3. **Configure Goal**:
   - **Type**: Steps, Distance, Calories, Weight, etc.
   - **Target**: Specific number to achieve
   - **Period**: Daily, Weekly, or Monthly
   - **Start Date**: When to begin tracking
4. **Save Goal**: Confirm and start tracking

#### Goal Types
- **Steps**: Daily step targets (e.g., 10,000 steps)
- **Distance**: Weekly distance goals (e.g., 50km per week)
- **Calories**: Daily calorie burn targets
- **Weight**: Target weight with timeline
- **Active Minutes**: Daily activity duration goals
- **Sleep**: Nightly sleep hour targets

#### Tracking Progress
- **Visual Progress**: Circular progress indicators
- **Streak Counter**: Days/weeks of consecutive achievement
- **Achievement Badges**: Rewards for milestones
- **Goal History**: Past performance and trends

### 🍎 Nutrition Tracking
Monitor your food intake and nutritional balance.

#### Logging Food
1. **Add Food Entry**:
   - Tap "+" on Nutrition page
   - Search food database or enter custom item
   - Select portion size
   - Choose meal type (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, Snack)
2. **Quick Add**:
   - Use barcode scanner for packaged foods
   - Save frequent foods to favorites
   - Create custom recipes

#### Nutrition Information
- **Calories**: Total daily caloric intake
- **Macronutrients**: Protein, Carbs, Fat breakdown
- **Micronutrients**: Vitamins and minerals (premium feature)
- **Fiber**: Daily fiber intake tracking
- **Sugar**: Added and natural sugar monitoring

#### Meal Planning
1. **Create Meal Plans**: Pre-plan daily meals
2. **Recipe Builder**: Create custom recipes with nutrition facts
3. **Shopping Lists**: Generate lists from meal plans
4. **Nutrition Goals**: Set targets for specific nutrients

### 😴 Sleep Tracking
Monitor sleep patterns and quality.

#### Sleep Data Entry
1. **Manual Entry**:
   - Tap Sleep section on Home page
   - Enter bedtime and wake time
   - Rate sleep quality (1-5 stars)
   - Add notes about sleep
2. **Automatic Tracking**: Connect compatible devices for auto-tracking

#### Sleep Insights
- **Sleep Duration**: Hours of sleep per night
- **Sleep Quality**: Subjective quality rating
- **Sleep Patterns**: Bedtime and wake time consistency
- **Weekly Average**: Average sleep duration over 7 days
- **Sleep Debt**: Cumulative sleep deficit tracking

### 📈 Analytics & Insights
Comprehensive data analysis and trends.

#### Health Trends
- **Progress Charts**: Visual representation of health metrics over time
- **Trend Analysis**: Identifying patterns in your data
- **Goal Achievement**: Success rates and improvement areas
- **Comparative Data**: Month-over-month comparisons

#### Data Export
1. **Export Options**: CSV, PDF, or share with healthcare providers
2. **Date Ranges**: Custom date range selection
3. **Metric Selection**: Choose specific data to export
4. **Sharing**: Email reports or save to cloud storage

### 👤 Profile & Settings
Customize your experience and manage account settings.

#### Personal Information
- **Basic Details**: Name, age, gender, height
- **Activity Level**: Sedentary to very active
- **Health Goals**: Weight loss, maintenance, muscle gain
- **Medical Info**: Conditions, medications (optional)

#### App Preferences
- **Units**: Metric vs Imperial measurements
- **Notifications**: Reminder settings and frequency
- **Privacy**: Data sharing and visibility settings
- **Theme**: Light, dark, or auto theme selection

#### Connected Services
1. **Link Devices**: 
   - Fitness trackers (Fitbit, Garmin, etc.)
   - Smartwatches (Apple Watch, Samsung Galaxy Watch)
   - Smart scales and blood pressure monitors
2. **Health Platforms**:
   - Google Fit integration
   - Apple Health sync
   - Samsung Health connection
3. **Sync Settings**: Automatic vs manual synchronization

## Tips for Success

### 🎯 Goal Setting Best Practices
- **Start Small**: Begin with achievable goals and gradually increase
- **Be Specific**: Set concrete, measurable targets
- **Regular Review**: Adjust goals based on progress and changing needs
- **Celebrate Wins**: Acknowledge achievements, both big and small

### 📱 Daily Usage Tips
- **Morning Check-in**: Review yesterday's progress and set today's intentions
- **Regular Logging**: Enter data consistently for accurate tracking
- **Weekly Reviews**: Analyze trends and adjust strategies
- **Stay Motivated**: Use achievement badges and progress visualization

### 🔄 Data Accuracy
- **Consistent Timing**: Log data at similar times each day
- **Honest Tracking**: Accurate logging leads to better insights
- **Device Calibration**: Ensure connected devices are properly calibrated
- **Manual Verification**: Double-check automatic data entries

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### App Not Syncing
1. **Check Internet**: Ensure stable internet connection
2. **Restart App**: Close and reopen the application
3. **Manual Sync**: Pull down on home screen to refresh
4. **Update App**: Install latest version from app store

#### Missing Data
1. **Check Date**: Verify you're viewing the correct date
2. **Device Connection**: Ensure connected devices are properly paired
3. **Permissions**: Check that app has necessary device permissions
4. **Backup Data**: Regular backups prevent data loss

#### Performance Issues
1. **Storage Space**: Ensure device has sufficient storage
2. **Background Apps**: Close unnecessary background applications
3. **App Updates**: Keep app updated to latest version
4. **Device Restart**: Restart device if performance persists
